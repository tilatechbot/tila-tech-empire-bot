# рҹӨ– TELEGRAM BOT BRIDGE SYSTEM\n\n> Complete Telegram to WhatsApp integration with core commands for account linking and status monitoring.\n\n## рҹ“Ӣ Overview\n\nThe Telegram Bridge Bot acts as a control center for managing your WhatsApp connection through Telegram.\n\n**Flow Architecture:**\n```\nUser (Telegram)\n      вҶ“\nTelegram Bot API\n      вҶ“\nNode.js Backend Server\n      вҶ“\nBaileys WhatsApp Session\n      вҶ“\nWhatsApp Network\n```\n\n---\n\n## рҹҡҖ Core Commands\n\n### 1пёҸвғЈ `/start` - Entry Point\n\n**Purpose:** Initializes bot session and displays welcome menu\n\n**System Action:**\n- Creates/loads user session\n- Initializes SaaS profile\n- Displays main menu with quick actions\n\n**Output:**\n```\nрҹ‘Ӣ Welcome to TILA Bridge Bot!\n\nHello [UserName]! рҹҺү\n\nThis bot bridges WhatsApp and Telegram allowing you to:\nвң… Connect your WhatsApp account\nвң… Monitor connection status\nвң… Manage your session\nвң… Use advanced features\n```\n\n**Inline Buttons:**\n- рҹ”— Connect WhatsApp\n- рҹ“І Pair Phone\n- рҹӨ– Bot Status\n- вқ“ Help\n- вҡҷпёҸ Settings\n- рҹ“– Menu\n\n---\n\n### 2пёҸвғЈ `/connect` - QR Code Linking\n\n**Purpose:** Starts WhatsApp QR code linking process\n\n**System Action:**\n1. Generates WhatsApp Baileys session request\n2. Creates QR code with session data\n3. Sends QR code as image\n4. Waits for WhatsApp scan confirmation\n\n**Output:**\n```\nрҹ”— Connecting WhatsApp...\nвҸі Generating QR code...\nрҹ“ұ Scan with WhatsApp on your phone\n```\n\n**Then:**\n```\nрҹ“ё WhatsApp QR Code\n\n1пёҸвғЈ Open WhatsApp on your phone\n2пёҸвғЈ Go to Settings вҶ’ Linked Devices\n3пёҸвғЈ Tap \"Link a Device\"\n4пёҸвғЈ Scan this QR code\n\nвҸұпёҸ QR code expires in 2 minutes\n```\n\n**Process:**\n1. User runs `/connect`\n2. Bot generates unique QR code\n3. User scans with WhatsApp\n4. Baileys captures session\n5. Connection established вң…\n\n---\n\n### 3пёҸвғЈ `/pair` - Phone Number Linking\n\n**Purpose:** Connect WhatsApp using phone number (no QR required)\n\n**System Action:**\n1. Prompts for WhatsApp phone number\n2. Validates format (+country code)\n3. Sends OTP to WhatsApp\n4. Requests OTP verification\n5. Links account automatically\n\n**Output:**\n```\nрҹ“І WhatsApp Phone Pairing\n\nThis method connects WhatsApp without QR code scanning.\n\nHow it works:\n1пёҸвғЈ Enter your WhatsApp phone number\n2пёҸвғЈ Receive OTP (One-Time Password)\n3пёҸвғЈ Confirm OTP\n4пёҸвғЈ Account linked! вң…\n\nFormat: +1234567890 (with country code)\n```\n\n**User Flow:**\n1. User sends phone number: `+1234567890`\n2. Bot validates format\n3. \"рҹ“І Sending OTP to WhatsApp...\" (2 seconds)\n4. \"вң… OTP Sent! Enter the 6-digit code\"\n5. User sends OTP: `123456`\n6. \"вң… WhatsApp Connected!\" вң…\n\n---\n\n### 4пёҸвғЈ `/status` - System Health Monitor\n\n**Purpose:** Checks bot and WhatsApp connection status\n\n**System Action:**\n- Reads backend session state\n- Checks WhatsApp connection health\n- Reports system metrics\n- Shows uptime & memory usage\n\n**Output:**\n```\nв•ӯв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв•®\nв”ғ  рҹӨ– SYSTEM STATUS\nв•°в”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв•Ҝ\n\nрҹ“ұ Bot Status:\n  рҹҹў Status: ONLINE\n  вҸұпёҸ Uptime: 2h 30m\n  рҹ’ҫ Memory: 125MB\n  рҹ“Ұ Version: v2.0.0\n\nрҹ“І WhatsApp Connection:\n  рҹҹў Status: CONNECTED\n  рҹ‘Ө User: John Doe\n  рҹҶ” JID: 1234567890@s.whatsapp.net\n\nвҡЎ Mode: ACTIVE\nрҹ”Ӣ Session: STABLE\n\nв•ӯв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв•®\nв”ғ  рҹ“Ҡ HEALTH CHECK\nв•°в”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв”Ғв•Ҝ\n\nвң… All systems operational!\n```\n\n**Inline Buttons:**\n- рҹ”— Connect WhatsApp\n- рҹ”„ Refresh\n- рҹ“– Menu\n- вқҢ Close\n\n---\n\n## рҹ“ұ Additional Commands\n\n### `/help` -
